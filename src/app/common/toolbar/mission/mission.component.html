Mission, Vision & Values

Building a nation of skilled, empowered, and globally competitive Filipinos.

Vision

The transformational leader in the technical education and skills development of the Filipino workforce.

Mission

TESDA sets direction, promulgates relevant standards, and implements programs geared towards a quality-assured and inclusive technical education, skills development, and certification system.

Core Values

  • Demonstrated competence
  • Institutional integrity
  • Personal commitment
  • Culture of innovativeness
  • Deep sense of nationalism

“TESDA continues to empower the Filipino workforce — transforming skills into opportunities and dreams into realities.”
